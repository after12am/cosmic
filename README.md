cosmic
======

Once upon a time there was a man. The man was absorbed in media art, and then met [robert hodgin's work](http://roberthodgin.com). His work was so amazing that words do not come out. That man won't get bored of watching his work forever. Sooner after that, that man started wanting to create a work like his. Then that man's work began.
